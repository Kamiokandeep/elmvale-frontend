Our History

The Elmvale and District Horticultural Society was founded in 1949 in Elmvale and the surrounding municipalities of Flos and Medonte, now part of the Township of Springwater.

Since its creation, our society has been dedicated to horticulture, floral artistry, environmental stewardship, and education.

Our Evolution

Recognizing our evolving role as educators and environmental advocates, we rebranded in 2019 as Elmvale District Garden Enthusiasts (E.D.G.E.).

This name reflects our mission to promote gardening, community beautification, and sustainability. Our floral emblem, the geranium, symbolizes our commitment to fostering growth and environmental awareness.

Our Mission & Objectives

At E.D.G.E., our mission is to:

Educate

The community about gardening, environmental preservation, and sustainability.

Encourage

The planting of trees, flowers, and shrubs to beautify our surroundings.

Promote

Civic beautification through garden projects and tree planting.

Support

Local students with bursaries to further their studies in horticulture.

Provide

Engaging events, workshops, and speakers to inspire and inform our members.

Our Commitment

Through our monthly meetings, workshops, and community projects, we strive to create a greener and more vibrant environment for everyone.

Bursaries

At Elmvale District Garden Enthusiasts (E.D.G.E.), we believe in supporting future generations of horticulturists and environmentalists. We offer:

E.D.G.E. Bursary

Amount:

$300

Eligibility:

Open to students from Elmvale High School pursuing post-secondary studies in horticulture, environmental sciences, or related fields.

How to Apply:

Applications are available through the high school's guidance office.

District 16 Dr. Raymond Ives Bursary

A scholarship opportunity provided by our district.
